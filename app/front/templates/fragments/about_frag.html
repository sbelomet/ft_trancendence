Welcome to Pongo 🎉

Greetings !

Here we are, standing tall, after all these years of hard work and perseverance! This was quite the arduous task, but like the brilliant geniuses that we are, no amount of stress, bad weather or pagan holidays could deter us from our goal.

Our features to get to 125% include:

  • ⚙️ Backend : Django is our man in the shadows, the brain of the operation.
  • 🎨 Frontend : Pure JavaScript was combined with Bootstrap to give you a visual.
  • 📊 Database : PostgreSQL keeps all the receipts, whether it be users, scores or your precious profile pics.
  • 👥 User managment : Secure accounts, stats, friendships, and login via 42 thanks to OAuth.
  • 💻 Gameplay : Remote players and real time chat thanks to Django Channels.
  • 🔒 Cybersecurity : Double authentication (2FA) and secure sessions with JWT.
  • 🎮 3D graphics : Cutting edge 3D graphics with Three.js.
  • 🏓 Server-side Pong : Complete takeover from the backend for all game calculations.
  • 🌐 Accessibility : Compatible with all browsers, wouldn't be fun otherwise.
